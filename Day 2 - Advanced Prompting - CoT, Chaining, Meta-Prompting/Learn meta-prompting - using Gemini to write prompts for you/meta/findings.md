# Meta-Prompting Findings

## 1. When Meta-Prompting Works Well

- When starting new client projects
- When you need structured prompts quickly
- When scaling prompt creation across teams
- When consistency is more important than creativity

---

## 2. When Manual Prompting is Better

- Simple tasks (one-step prompts)
- Highly creative tasks (ads, storytelling)
- When domain knowledge is very specific
- When fast iteration is needed

---

## 3. Key Observation

Meta-prompting improves:
- structure
- consistency
- completeness

But sometimes reduces:
- creativity
- flexibility

---

## 4. Final Conclusion

Meta-prompting is best used as a **prompt generator tool**, not a replacement for expert prompt writing.

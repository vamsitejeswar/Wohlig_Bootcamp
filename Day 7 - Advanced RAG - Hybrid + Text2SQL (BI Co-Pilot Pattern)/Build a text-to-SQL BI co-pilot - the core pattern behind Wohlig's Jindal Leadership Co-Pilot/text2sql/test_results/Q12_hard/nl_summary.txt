The percentage of trips exceeding 5 miles varies significantly throughout the day, peaking in the early morning hours. Specifically, nearly 60% of trips taken at 5 AM are longer than 5 miles, suggesting a prevalence of longer commutes or journeys at that time. Conversely, the late afternoon and evening hours, particularly at 6 PM, see the lowest proportion of longer trips, with only about 21% exceeding 5 miles.

# Why Each Section Matters

## Role
Defines the expertise and perspective of the model.
Without it, responses are generic.

## Context
Provides background information.
Without it, the model may misunderstand the domain.

## Task
Clearly defines the required action.
Without it, outputs become inconsistent.

## Constraints
Controls length and behavior.
Without it, responses may be verbose or inaccurate.

## Output Format
Ensures consistent structure.
Without it, every response may look different.

## Examples
Demonstrates the desired pattern.
Without it, output quality can vary significantly.

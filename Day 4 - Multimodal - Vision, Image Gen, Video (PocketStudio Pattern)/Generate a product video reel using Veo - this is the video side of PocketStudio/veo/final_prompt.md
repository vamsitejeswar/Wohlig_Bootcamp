# Final Prompt (Annotated)

**Product Script:** Meet the future. The iPhone 16 Pro, crafted in titanium for ultimate strength and elegance. Unmatched performance, stunning photography. Experience the next generation. Order yours today.

## The Best Prompt
```
Cinematic product showcase of an iPhone 16 Pro. Opens with a slow dolly-in from a low angle, then smooth pan across the phone's titanium edge. Golden hour lighting with warm rim light highlighting the contours. Shallow depth of field with bokeh background. Final shot: slow pull-out revealing the full phone on a reflective glass surface. Mood: premium, sleek, innovative.
```

| Phrase | Why it works |
|--------|-------------|
| `Cinematic` | Sets the overall visual quality level |
| `slow dolly-in` | Specific camera move - builds anticipation |
| `low angle` | Makes product look heroic |
| `smooth pan across` | Reveals texture and detail |
| `Golden hour lighting` | Warm, premium, natural light feel |
| `warm rim light` | Separates product from background |
| `Shallow depth of field` | Focuses attention, creates bokeh |
| `slow pull-out` | Natural ending, reveals full product |
| `reflective glass surface` | Adds sophistication |
| `premium, sleek, innovative` | Mood keywords guide the model's style |

# Brand Guidelines

Brand Positioning:
Premium
Modern
Aspirational
Trustworthy

Allowed Colors:
- White
- Beige
- Warm Gold
- Soft Grey

Allowed Visuals:
- Studio lighting
- Premium lifestyle environments
- Soft shadows
- Natural reflections

Prohibited Elements:
- Alcohol
- Cigarettes
- Weapons
- Religious symbols
- Political symbols
- Explicit content

Requirements:
- Product must remain unchanged.
- Product logo must remain visible.
- Product color should not change.
- Product should occupy at least 40% of frame.
- Output should look realistic.
